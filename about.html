about me

From afar, the Earth may seem blue and silent, but to me, every pixel and every coordinate represents an opportunity to decode and design. My education in Geomatics Engineering at Istanbul Technical University has equipped me not only with technical expertise but also with the ability to think systematically, create solutions, and transform raw data into meaningful insights. No matter the field I work in, my goal is to be a forward-thinking, visionary engineer who constantly strives to stay ahead of the curve. I don’t just use technology—I reshape it, reinterpret it, and bring fresh perspectives to the table. Because engineering is not only about understanding the past—it's about having the courage to reimagine the future.

Human-Centered & Spatial Solutions

I develop integrated technological solutions by considering human life, environmental conditions, and spatial realities together. Whether it’s child-friendly city analyses or unmanned ground vehicles designed for post-disaster response, my priority has always been human-centered thinking. Because engineering is not just about calculation—it's also about responsibility.

Engineering That Delivers Results

In every project I’ve developed, I’ve focused not only on theory but on producing applicable and sustainable solutions in the field. Through leading the ITU RAKE team, managing projects worth hundreds of thousands of lira, achieving success in international competitions, and gaining hands-on field experience, I’ve committed to producing real outcomes—not just promises.

An Innovative Perspective

By working in areas such as advanced image processing, digital mapping, digital twins, and autonomous systems, I’ve consistently aimed to push beyond the conventional boundaries of engineering. In multidisciplinary projects, I merge creativity with technical knowledge, striving to develop unconventional and forward-thinking solutions.
